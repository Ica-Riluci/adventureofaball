What the game is about?

Your hero is an ordinary ball wanting to explore a geometry world. The longer distance you travel, the higher points you get. By avoiding traps and seizing bonus, you could improve your performance. By collecting fragments of armors, you can switch to new form and equip your hero with new skills.

Introduction to XP System

For every skill you own, you accumulate experience(XP) for stronger ablities. Dusts are props that help you obtain XP points.

Every normal dust will reward you 50 bonus points and 1 xp points for your current hero.

Introduction to Terrain System

There are several kinds of terrains which are listed below: plains, walls and firelands.

When you bump into walls or step into firelands, you will be hurt. Bumping into a side wall or stepping into firelands, the damage will be a certain value d or your hit points left if your have hit points no more than d. Besides, bumping into walls just in front of you invites death.

Introduction to Hero System

Preliminarily, you are given a plain hero [Noob], which has no skill. By collecting dusts of armor which has no bonus point, you are able to activate new armors. If your hero of this form is already activated, it will count as 10 xp points for this armor.

Samurai
